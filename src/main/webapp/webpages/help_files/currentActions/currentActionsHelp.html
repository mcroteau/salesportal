About Current Actions

Current Actions will show you Sales Actions that are due today, as well as passed due. If your a Sales Manager or Service Manager of a Team of Salesmen and would like to view your Salesmen's Sales Actions that are due today or passed due, you will be able to view them here as well.

  • Viewing Passed Due Sales Actions
  • Sales Actions by Territory
  • Sales Action Alarm Popup

Viewing Passed Due Sales Actions

From this screen you will be able to view Current Actions. These include Sales Actions which are due today as well passed due Sales Actions(S.A.).

By default, this screen will only show Today's Sales Actions for your Territories. However, you can view passed due S.A. by clicking on "Include Passed Due Sales Actions"

You can click "Include Passed Due Sales Actions" for any of the Territory options (Your Territory, Sales Manager Territories, Service Manager Territories).

Sales Action Alarm

With "Current Actions" you are given the ability to popup a seperate window that runs even while your Sales Portal session is closed. When opened, the Sales Action Alarm will update and within 15 minutes (default settings) of a Sales Action being due, an alarm noise ("GONG") will be activated. To activate the Sales Action Alarm, click:

Once opened, you will see a condensed view of your *Sales Actions Due Today. You can disable the gong noise at any time by selecting the "Turn Off Alert" checkbox.

*NOTE: The Sales Action Alarm only displays and works for Sales Actions for Prospects in your Territory. These include all Territorie Where you are specified as a "Territory Owner". This will not show Sales Manager Territory Sales Actions nor Service Manager Territory Sales Actions
